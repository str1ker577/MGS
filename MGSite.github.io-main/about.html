About Us

Mapúa Gaming Society (MGS), is a thriving gaming organization at Mapua University. It promotes the growing potential and value of esports and gaming while emphasizing academic balance. MGS is a platform that connects students to collaborate, establish camaraderie, and encourage discipline, skills development, and self-regulated learning.

MGS is a platform for students to connect, collaborate, and celebrate their love for gaming, that builds an environment to establish discipline, camaraderie, self-regulated learning, and skills development among the students of Mapúa University.

We give opportunities to gamers in:

- Esports Tournaments: Competing & Organizing
- Community Events: Game Nights & Conventions
- Content Creation: Streaming, & Shoutcasting
- And So Much More!

Our Mission

Mapúa Gaming Society will be an organization committed to providing students exposure to different industries related to gaming, competitive esports, and leadership, giving them their avenue of growth and realizing their true potential. Mapúa Gaming Society seeks to promote a healthy balance in academics and excel towards the advancements in the gaming industry while empowering the principles of a good role model to society and future generations.

Our Vision

Mapúa Gaming Society aims to be the leading gaming organization in Mapúa University, producing competitive, critical thinkers and proficient and effective communicators in the local and international gaming industry, community, and esports.
